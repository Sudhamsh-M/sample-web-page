About AI Week

AI Week is a premier event dedicated to showcasing the latest advancements and applications in Artificial Intelligence. Join us for a series of engaging activities designed to inspire, educate, and connect AI enthusiasts, students, and professionals. Our major events include:

  • AI Hackathon: A 24-hour coding challenge to solve real-world problems using AI.
  • Keynote Speeches: Insights from leading AI researchers and industry experts.
  • Workshops: Hands-on sessions covering topics like Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and NLP.
  • Project Showcase: An opportunity for students to present their innovative AI projects.
